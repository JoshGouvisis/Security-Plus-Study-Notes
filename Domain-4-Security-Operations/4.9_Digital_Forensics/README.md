# 4.9 Digital Forensics

* **Order of Volatility:** The sequence in which digital evidence must be captured before it disappears:
    1.  **CPU Cache/Registers** (Most Volatile)
    2.  **RAM** (System Memory)
    3.  **Swap/Pagefile** (Temporary storage on disk)
    4.  **Hard Drive/Disk** (Least Volatile)
* **Chain of Custody:** A chronological document/log showing everyone who handled the evidence to ensure it remains admissible in court.
* **Legal Hold:** A formal requirement to preserve all relevant data (no deleting or overwriting) when litigation or an investigation is expected.
* **Provenance:** Tracking the origin and history of a piece of data to ensure its integrity.

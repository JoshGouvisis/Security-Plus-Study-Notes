## 4.2 Asset Management
 
### Acquisition and Assignment
 
- Acquisition: formal procurement. Request, budget approval, negotiate terms, purchase, invoice, payment.
- Assignment/accounting: central asset tracking system. Associate every asset with an owner. Classify by type (hardware, software). Assign barcodes, RFID tags, or asset numbers.
- Monitoring/enumeration: inventory all assets including components (CPU, memory, storage). Associate support tickets with specific devices. Enumerate all parts.
### Disposal and Decommissioning
 
- Media sanitization: securely wipe or destroy data before reuse. Once done correctly, data cannot be recovered with forensic tools.
- Physical destruction: shredder, pulverizer, drill, hammer, degaussing (removes magnetic field, destroys the drive), incineration.
- Certificate of destruction: third-party confirmation that destruction was completed. Required paper trail for compliance.
- Data retention: regulatory compliance drives retention periods (emails, financial records vary by regulation). Operational needs include accidental deletion recovery and disaster recovery. Differentiate retention rules by data type and application.

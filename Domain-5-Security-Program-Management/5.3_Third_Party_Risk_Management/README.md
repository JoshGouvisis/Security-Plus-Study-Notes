# 5.3 Third-Party Risk Management
* **Supply Chain Risk:** Evaluating vulnerabilities in hardware, software, or service providers.
* **Agreements:**
    * **SLA (Service Level Agreement):** Defines measurable performance and uptime.
    * **MOU (Memo of Understanding):** Outlines a mutual intent without a legal contract.
    * **NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement):** Protects proprietary data.
